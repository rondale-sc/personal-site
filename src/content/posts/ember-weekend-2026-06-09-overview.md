Ember Weekend was a show I ran from 2015 to 2019 with Chase McCarthy and Robert Jackson. It focused on Ember — the framework — as a source of news and goings-on in the Ember ecosystem.

All episodes are archived on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@emberweekend).
